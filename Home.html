Adult requirements and shots

To ensure your photo meets the regulations of a New Zealand passport, the following image requirements should be met.

1. Neutral expression, not smiling or frowning

2. Hair not covering any face features, showing all eyebrows

3. No hair clip showing

4. No head bands, or head coverings

5. Must not wear glasses and hats

6. Eyes must be open

7. Mouth closed

8. Taken with a plain background (e.g. no people, no objects)

9. Hair behind ears or tied up

10. Head straight to the camera

11. Must not have shadows on face or background

14. In focus, with no red-eye and no reflected light on the face
